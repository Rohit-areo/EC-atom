Cyber Security

What is Cyber security

Cybersecurity, or information security, is the practice of protecting computer systems, networks, and
data from theft, damage, or unauthorized access. It encompasses a wide range of technologies, processes,
and practices designed to safeguard digital information and ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of data.

Cybersecurity Roadmap

Key Milestones

Phase 1: Assess

Conduct a thorough cybersecurity assessment to identify weaknesses and vulnerabilities.

Phase 2: Plan

Create a comprehensive cybersecurity plan outlining strategies, policies, and resources.

Phase 3: Implement

Implement security measures, including firewalls, antivirus, and encryption tools.

Phase 4: Monitor

Establish continuous monitoring processes to detect and respond to security incidents.

Phase 5: Improve

Regularly review and update cybersecurity measures based on evolving threats and technologies.
